Featured Projects

Globe Maritime Training Center [MTI]

A centralized Maritime Training Management System that streamlines enrollment, scheduling, and internal operations within the training center. It features role-based access for Admin, Registrar, Accounting, Marketing, and Training, allowing efficient management of trainees, courses, and reports. The system ensures smooth coordination across departments and a seamless experience from registration to course completion.

Sevendays Coffee - Angat

A comprehensive Point-of-Sale (POS) system designed for coffee shops, featuring real-time inventory and product management, seamless order tracking, and secure payment processing for both in-store and online transactions. The system enables baristas and staff to efficiently handle orders, monitor stock levels, and generate insightful sales reports, while customers enjoy a smooth checkout experience through multiple payment options—whether at the counter or via the web platform.

IncluLearn

IncluLearn is an inclusive learning management system (LMS) designed to support young learners aged 6–8 (Grade 1-3) who manifest Dyslexia and Dyscalculia. It features adaptive course management, progress monitoring, and accessible assessment tools suited for both in-person and online learning environments. The platform empowers educators to deliver personalized instruction and track individual development, while students benefit from an engaging, supportive, and easy-to-navigate learning experience that accommodates their unique learning needs—whether in the classroom or through the web platform.

Snap&Shoot

A comprehensive e-commerce platform designed for businesses, featuring real-time product and inventory management, customer engagement tools, and secure payment processing for both in-store and online transactions. The system allows merchants to efficiently manage products, track sales performance, and analyze consumer behavior, while customers enjoy a smooth and convenient shopping experience through multiple access points—whether visiting the physical store or browsing via the web platform.

Trustees

Trustees is an e‑commerce clothing store focused on T‑shirts and apparel. It features a rich product catalog with size and color variants, product customization options, and a smart inventory system that syncs stock in real time. Customers enjoy intuitive product filters, size guides, secure checkout with multiple payment options, order tracking, and seamless returns; merchants get order management, promotional tools (discount codes, bundles), and sales analytics to optimize assortment and fulfillment. The platform is mobile‑first, accessible, and built for both local retail and online storefront operations.
